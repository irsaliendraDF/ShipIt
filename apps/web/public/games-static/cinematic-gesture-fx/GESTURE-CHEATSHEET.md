App: **http://localhost:8080**

Grant camera access when prompted. When a hand is detected you'll see a soft blue glow at your palm — that confirms tracking is working.

2. Clap them together

## Single-hand gestures

> Tip: hold the pose steady for a moment. If the effect doesn't fire, check that your whole hand is visible in the frame.

### Open Palm Forward — Iron Man Repulsor
**Pose**: all 5 fingers extended, palm facing the camera.
- Start from a fist, then open every finger wide
- Tilt your palm so it points at the lens
- Keep the hand steady for half a second

**Effect**: bright **aperture ring** glows on your palm + thin **white-hot filament** down the center of a translucent blue **cone** + palm flash + camera shake. Four visible layers all follow your hand together.

### Web Shooter — Spider-Man Web
**Pose**: thumb + index + middle extended, ring + pinky curled.
- Make a pistol/finger-gun shape
- Extend your middle finger too so three fingers stick out
- Curl the ring and pinky tight into your palm

**Effect**: white-blue **web line** with a slight sag + **bright anchor node** at your wrist where the web originates + **3 cross-strands** perpendicular to the line, giving it a cobweb lattice look. Fades after ~1.5s.

### Fist → Open (quick snap) — Shockwave Burst
**Pose**: transition from a closed fist to a fully open hand within about 1/3 of a second.
1. Make a tight fist
2. Snap all 5 fingers open at once

**Effect**: **primary particle explosion** + **smoke/dust ring** that lingers + **shockwave ring** at impact + a **secondary smaller burst** ~0.25s later for a follow-up pop.

### Pinch & Draw — Dr. Strange Green Portal
**Pose**: thumb + index finger touching, then move your hand outward.
1. Touch your thumb and index fingertip together (pinch)
2. Keep them touching
3. Move your hand sideways, up, or diagonally — like drawing a circle in the air

**Effect**: emerald ring **draws itself open** over 0.35s at the midpoint of your motion. **Inner rune ring** at ~72% radius counter-rotates. **8 rune dots** orbit around the outer rim. **Circulating sparks** fan around the ring. Rising **green embers** inside the disc.

### Open Palm Swipe — Particle Slash
**Pose**: open hand moving fast sideways across the camera view.
- Keep all 5 fingers extended, palm somewhat toward camera
- Swipe quickly left-to-right or right-to-left
- Must be horizontal and fast

**Effect**: a **crisp core edge line** draws the slash across, trailed by a **fan of gold particles** along the swipe direction.

### Palm Up (held 1 second) — Floating Holo Orb
**Pose**: all 5 fingers extended, palm facing **up** at the ceiling.
1. Hold your hand flat, palm up like you're holding a tray
2. Hold for a full second

**Effect**: a **glowing blue orb with fresnel rim** + a **bright white nucleus** at its center + a **tilted orbiting ring** spinning around it. Gently bobs and follows your hand.

## Two-hand gestures

> These need both hands visible with good confidence. Keep both in frame.

### JARVIS HUD — Two L-Shapes
**Pose**: both hands make an "L" (thumb and index extended like a gun, other fingers curled).
- Left hand: L shape
- Right hand: L shape
- Hold them out with some space between them

**Effect**: 3 concentric **rotating rings** on different axes + 36 **tick marks** around the outer ring + **scanning sweep line** + 6 **glyph sprites** + **central pulsing data core dot** + vertical **"data readout" column** of flickering horizontal lines. Scales larger the farther apart your hands are.

### Double Palms Forward — Wide Light Beam
**Pose**: both palms facing the camera, all fingers extended (same as open-palm-forward but with both hands).

**Effect**: wide volumetric **light cone** with dust motes + **bright lens-flare disc** at the source + **spinning rim ring** around the source.

### Shield — Palms Forward, Fingers Curled
**Pose**: both palms toward camera, but fingers only slightly curled (a "catcher's mitt" pose). Hands side by side at similar height, not too far apart.

**Effect**: ornate **runic shield disc** with angular stripes + **secondary tilted inner torus ring** counter-rotating + **4 pulsing rune dots** at cardinal compass points around the shield.

### Whip — Both Fists, Arms Wide
**Pose**: both hands in tight fists, held far apart (wide stance).

**Effect**: two **physics-based whip segments** trail from each fist with spring-follow lag + **bright tip-glow spheres** at the end of each whip, pulsing. Move your arms and the whips crack around.

### Charge Orb — Open Palms Facing Each Other
**Pose**: both hands open, fingers spread, palms facing each other (like holding an invisible ball).

**Effect**: glowing **charging orb** with fresnel aura + **inner white nucleus** + **6 spark satellites** orbiting around it in 3D. Spreading your hands grows the whole thing.

### Prayer — Palms Pressed Together
**Pose**: palms pressed together in prayer, fingers pointing up.

**Effect**: vertical **volumetric golden pillar** shoots upward, slowly rotating + **base ring halo** at your hands + **cap ring halo** at the top + rising embers inside.

### Crossed Arms — Reality Warp
**Pose**: cross your arms in front of your chest — left wrist on the right side, right wrist on the left side.

**Effect**: dark purple **ripple disc** radiates outward + **inner void disc** at the center (reality "tearing") + **80 debris particles** flung outward with gravity.

### Cup Hands — Energy Ball Charge
**Pose**: both hands in a cup/claw shape, palms facing each other, fingers curled inward (tighter than charge orb). Hands close together.
1. Cup both hands like you're holding a small ball
2. Keep them close
3. Slowly spread them apart to grow the ball bigger
4. **Pull apart fast to LAUNCH** the energy ball forward

**Effect**: **layered energy ball**: bright fresnel aura + inner **white nucleus** pulsing at the dead center + thin **equatorial ring** spinning on a tilted axis + **40 orbiting particles** + occasional **crackling lightning arcs** + **inward-spiraling dust inflow** during charge. Snap hands apart to launch — it flies forward with a sinusoidal wobble and detonates on impact with a shockwave + 420-particle burst.

## Combos (chain two or three gestures)

Do these fast — each combo has a time window. The on-screen chain strip at the bottom shows your recent gestures so you can see your progress.

### Repulsor Barrage — Three Blasts
Do: **Open palm forward × 3** with the same hand in under 1.2 seconds.
- Push palm out, pull back, push out, pull back, push out.
**Effect**: three escalating repulsor blasts, last one with heavy shake.

### Portal Punch — Portal then Fist
Do: **Pinch & pull → clenched fist** with the same hand in under 0.8 seconds.
1. Pinch + draw to open a portal
2. Immediately clench that hand into a fist
**Effect**: portal opens, fist thrusts through, shockwave blows out the far side.

### Web Zip — Web then Swipe
Do: **Web shooter pose → fast open-palm swipe** with the same hand in under 0.6 seconds.
1. Make the web shooter pose
2. Open your hand and swipe it across fast
**Effect**: a long web line whips across the screen with a motion-blur trail.

### Strange Sanctum — Two Portals
Do: **Pinch & pull with left hand, then with right hand** within 0.5 seconds.
**Effect**: two portals open simultaneously, connected by an energy link with flowing sparks.

### Unibeam — Charge Then Release
Do: **Charge up (palms facing each other) held 1.5 seconds → both palms snap forward**.
1. Open palms facing each other, close together
2. Hold for 1.5 full seconds
3. Rotate both hands to face the camera
**Effect**: a massive horizontal unibeam with screen vignette.

### JARVIS Boot — L-Frame Expands
Do: **Two L-frames → both palms forward** within 1 second.
1. Two L-shapes
2. Open both hands flat toward the camera
**Effect**: full-screen JARVIS HUD with multiple rings, tick ladder, corner brackets.

### Cataclysm — Shield then Crossed Arms
Do: **Shield pose → crossed arms** within 2 seconds.
1. Palms forward with fingers curled (shield)
2. Cross your arms at your chest
**Effect**: full-screen reality warp + massive particle explosion. The nuclear option.

## Custom gestures — press `T` to open the Trainer

You can record your own poses and bind them to any effect.

### To record a new gesture
1. Press `T` to open the Trainer panel
2. Click **+ Record New Gesture**
3. Type a name (example: "Kamehameha")
4. Type an effect ID (example: `repulsor`, `portal`, `energyball` — full list shown in the prompt)
5. After a 3-2-1 countdown, **hold your pose steady for about 1 second**
6. Done — your gesture is saved

### To tune a gesture
- Each saved gesture has a **confidence slider** — higher = stricter match
- While the Trainer is open, hold your pose and watch the live **match: XX%** meter
- Lower the slider just below where your best matches are so the gesture fires reliably

### Quick-record shortcut
- Hold the pose you want
- Press `Shift + T`
- The recorder starts instantly with a default name

### Share gesture packs
- **Export JSON** button downloads all your gestures as a file
- **Import JSON** loads someone else's pack

### Three example gestures are pre-installed
Open Palm, Thumbs Up, L Shape — feel free to delete and record your own.

## Settings — press `,` (comma)

Five tabs:

**General** — webcam resolution readout, quality tier (Low/Medium/High/Ultra), mirror toggle, debug toggle.

**Gestures** — sensitivity sliders:
- **Single-hand stable frames** — lower = faster response, higher = more reliable
- **Two-hand stable frames** — same idea for two-hand poses
- **One-Euro smoothing** — reduces jitter but adds tiny delay

**Effects** — all the post-processing:
- **Sharpening** — makes the webcam look crisper (0.65 default; try 1.0)
- **Bloom strength / radius / threshold** — how intense effects glow
- **Film grain / chromatic aberration** — cinematic post-fx
- **Video contrast / brightness / saturation** — tune the webcam look
- **Pixel trail enabled** — voxel cubes at fingertips

**Preset** — switch style presets manually.

**Custom** — link to the Trainer.

All settings persist automatically. There's a **Reset to defaults** button at the bottom.

## Troubleshooting

### Gestures not firing
- Make sure your **whole hand** is in the camera view, fingers visible
- Try better lighting — side light on hands works best, avoid bright windows behind you
- In Settings → Gestures, lower **Single-hand stable frames** to 1 for instant triggering

### Effects flicker on/off while moving
- This means the pose briefly drops during movement
- In Settings → Gestures, raise **Grace frames** if exposed, OR slow your movement slightly
- The palm glow shows what's tracked — if the glow flickers off, that's a tracking issue not a gesture issue

### Two similar gestures firing each other
- Make your pose more distinct — curl unused fingers tighter into your palm
- The detector now picks the single most-specific match per hand, but very loose poses can still trigger the wrong one

### Camera looks too bright / too dark
- Settings → Effects → **Video brightness** (0.5 for darker, 1.0 for brighter)
- Settings → Effects → **Video contrast** (higher = hands pop more from background)

### Frame rate drops
- Settings → General → **Quality tier** → drop to Medium or Low
- Or reduce **Bloom strength** (biggest single performance win)

### The portal won't open
- Pinch has to be a **real touch** — thumb tip physically against index tip
- Then you have to **move the hand** while still pinching — any direction, at least a few inches

### Energy ball won't launch
- Hands have to be **cupped** (fingers curled inward, palms facing each other)
- To fire: **separate your hands quickly** — a deliberate outward pull, not a slow drift
